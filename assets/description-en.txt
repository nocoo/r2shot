R2Shot — One-Click Screenshot to Cloudflare R2

Capture any browser tab with a single click, instantly upload to your Cloudflare R2 bucket, and get a CDN URL copied to your clipboard. Perfect for developers, designers, and anyone who needs fast, reliable screenshot hosting.

FEATURES

- One-click capture — screenshot the visible area of any tab from your browser toolbar
- Cloudflare R2 upload — automatic upload via S3-compatible API with organized date-folder/GUID naming
- CDN URL — generates a public URL from your custom domain and copies it to clipboard with visual feedback
- Smart endpoint parsing — paste a full S3 API URL and R2Shot auto-extracts the endpoint and bucket name
- Connection testing — verify your R2 credentials work before saving
- Theme support — choose between light, dark, or system theme
- Configurable quality — adjust JPG compression from 1 to 100
- Multilingual — supports 10 languages: English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese (BR), Russian

HOW IT WORKS

1. Click the R2Shot icon in your browser toolbar
2. Press "Capture" to screenshot the current tab
3. The screenshot is automatically uploaded to your Cloudflare R2 bucket
4. The CDN URL is ready to copy with one click

SETUP

1. Install the extension
2. Open R2Shot Settings (click the gear icon in the popup)
3. Enter your Cloudflare R2 credentials:
   - Endpoint URL (S3-compatible)
   - Access Key ID
   - Secret Access Key
   - Bucket Name
   - Custom Domain (for CDN URLs)
4. Click "Test Connection" to verify
5. Save your settings — you're ready to go!

PERMISSIONS

- activeTab: Required to capture screenshots of the current tab
- storage: Required to save your R2 configuration locally

PRIVACY

R2Shot works entirely on your device. No data is collected, tracked, or shared with third parties. Screenshots are uploaded only to your own Cloudflare R2 bucket using credentials you provide. All configuration is stored locally in your browser.
